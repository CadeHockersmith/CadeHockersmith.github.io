Methods of entertainment

Entertain yourself to many of the vast activities that go around daily in Ecuador.

Surfing is a popular pastime in this region because of the proximity to the coast. You can enjoy learning to surf at a beach full of unique people and shops. Surfable waves are available in Ecuador year-round, so you won't need to worry too much about planning ahead. The comfortable weather ensures that your surfing experience will be one to remember. Surf tourism is very important to the local economy which provides you with a ton of great deals. Lodging and food near the coast is very affordable.

The coast of the Galapagos Islands is a great attraction for tourists. The island contains unique wildlife not found elsewhere in the world. The Galapagos islands are best known for their amazing array of species unique to the region. You can look around the island that fascinated Darwin with its incredible diversity of wildlife. The beautiful landscape is constantly changing due to intense volcanic activity. Eruptions and earthquakes are common is the region.

Those who enjoy shopping will love the huge Otavalo market. The Otavalo market is attractive to visitors for both its outstanding shopping and its cultural significance. We recommend visiting market on Saturday, which features the most activity from the various merchants there. It is crucial that you come early to find the best deals before they are gone. The Otavalo market is a fascinating way to experience traditional Ecuadorian culture and the traditions of the Andes.
