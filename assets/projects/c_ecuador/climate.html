How's the weather here?

Weather changes everywhere, find out what the weather you can expect here.

The country of Ecuador alternates between two extremes of wet and dryness. Ecuador is extremely close to the Equator which explains its intense variety in seasons. Be sure to plan ahead on your trip to Ecuador! The average temperature during the day is 66 °F (19 °C), which generally falls to an average of 50 °F (10 °C) at night.

Coastal areas are affected by ocean currents and between January and April are hot and rainy. Ecuador lies directly on the equator, so the entire country enjoys 12 hours of direct equatorial daylight 365 days a year. Most climates are in Ecuador are between these two extremes.
